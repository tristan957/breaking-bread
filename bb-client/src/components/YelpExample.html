    Caught the La Reina Del Sur Tacos Truck today on John Crow Blvd. at the Bright Football Complex, at noon. (Street named after our previous to Johnny Manzeil aka Johnny Football's Heisman Trophy winner).

    I asked how often they are there and they have been every day for the last two weeks. It's a public lot open to students. The tacos are 100% authentic Mexican street and while some people act like there's only a few food trucks at present, taco trucks have been servicing A&M University for 27 years. One of the originals still visits now and then.

    The tacos I had were hot and killer. Just ask for the beef steak on corn with onions and cilantro, period. Don't ask for any other ingredients as that's not normal. Beef steak on corn, onions, and cilantro. That's it. You'll thank me for this advice.
